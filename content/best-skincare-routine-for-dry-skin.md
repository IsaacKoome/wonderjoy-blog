- Glycerin

## 🌿 Best Ingredients for Dry Skin

When choosing products, look for these key ingredients:

- **Hyaluronic Acid** – deeply hydrates and plumps skin  
- **Ceramides** – repair and strengthen the skin barrier  
- **Glycerin** – draws moisture into the skin  
- **Shea Butter** – provides deep nourishment  

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

Many people unknowingly make dry skin worse. Avoid these:

- Using hot water on your face  
- Skipping moisturizer  
- Using harsh or alcohol-based products  
- Over-exfoliating  

---

## ⏳ When Will You See Results?

Dry skin doesn’t improve overnight, but with consistency you can expect:

- Softer skin within a few days  
- Reduced flakiness in 1–2 weeks  
- Healthy glow in 3–4 weeks  

Consistency is key—stick to your routine.

---

## 💡 Extra Tips for Dry Skin

- Apply moisturizer on slightly damp skin  
- Use a humidifier if your environment is dry  
- Avoid long, hot showers  

---

## 🧠 Final Thoughts

Dry skin needs gentle care, deep hydration, and consistency. You don’t need a complicated routine—just the right products used correctly.

Stick to the basics, avoid common mistakes, and your skin will gradually become healthier and more radiant.
