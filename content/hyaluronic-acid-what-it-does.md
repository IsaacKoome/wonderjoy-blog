---

## 🧪 What Is Hyaluronic Acid?

Hyaluronic acid is a naturally occurring substance in your body that helps retain moisture.

In skincare, it works by:

- Attracting water to your skin  
- Keeping your skin hydrated  
- Improving elasticity  
- Giving your skin a plump appearance  

---

## ✨ Benefits of Hyaluronic Acid

### 1. Deep Hydration

It can hold up to 1000 times its weight in water, making it one of the best hydrating ingredients available.

---

### 2. Plumper, Smoother Skin

Well-hydrated skin looks fuller, smoother, and more youthful.

---

### 3. Reduces Fine Lines

Hydration helps reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

---

### 4. Works for All Skin Types

- Dry skin → deeply hydrates  
- Oily skin → lightweight hydration  
- Sensitive skin → gentle and non-irritating

## ⚠️ Possible Side Effects

Hyaluronic acid is very safe, but:

- Using it on completely dry skin may reduce effectiveness  
- Low-quality products may feel sticky  

👉 Tip: Always apply on slightly damp skin.

## 🪜 How to Use Hyaluronic Acid

### Step-by-step:

1. Cleanse your face  
2. Apply hyaluronic acid on damp skin  
3. Follow with moisturizer  
4. Apply sunscreen (morning routine)  

---

### 🕒 When to use it?

- Morning ✅  
- Night ✅  
- Or both  

---

### 🔄 Can you combine it with other ingredients?

Yes—it works well with almost everything:

- Niacinamide  
- Vitamin C  
- Retinol  

👉 It actually helps reduce irritation from stronger ingredients.

## ❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Skipping moisturizer afterward  
- Expecting instant dramatic results
