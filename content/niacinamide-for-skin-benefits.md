## 🧪 What Is Niacinamide?

Niacinamide is a form of vitamin B3 that helps improve your skin’s overall health.

It works by:

- Strengthening your skin barrier  
- Reducing inflammation  
- Controlling oil production  
- Improving skin texture  

---

## ✨ Benefits of Niacinamide for Skin

### 1. Reduces Acne

Niacinamide helps calm inflammation and reduce breakouts, making it great for acne-prone skin.

---

### 2. Fades Dark Spots

It helps even out your skin tone and reduce hyperpigmentation over time.

---

### 3. Controls Oil Production

Perfect for oily skin—it reduces excess sebum without drying your skin.

---

### 4. Improves Skin Barrier

Stronger skin = less irritation and better moisture retention.

## ⚠️ Possible Side Effects

Niacinamide is generally safe, but in rare cases:

- Mild irritation  
- Redness (if concentration is too high)  

👉 Tip: Start with lower concentrations (2–5%) before moving to stronger formulas.

## 🪜 How to Use Niacinamide Correctly

### Step-by-step:

1. Cleanse your face  
2. Apply niacinamide serum  
3. Moisturize  
4. Apply sunscreen (morning routine)  

---

### 🕒 When to use it?

- Morning ✅  
- Night ✅  
- Or both  

---

### 🔄 Can you mix it with other ingredients?

Yes, niacinamide works well with:

- Hyaluronic acid  
- Salicylic acid  
- Retinol (use carefully)  

---

## ❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Using too high concentration too quickly  
- Applying too many active ingredients at once  
- Expecting instant results  

---

## ⏳ When Will You See Results?

Most people notice:

- Less oil in 1–2 weeks  
- Reduced acne in 3–4 weeks  
- Brighter skin in 4–8 weeks  

Consistency is key.
