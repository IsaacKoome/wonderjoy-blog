Stress does not create acne from nothing, but studies and clinical guidance link stressful school periods with worse breakouts in some teenagers. Stress can influence inflammation, sleep and habits: touching the face, picking, skipping cleansing or reaching for several emergency products.

**During exam week, the best skincare plan is the one that still happens when energy is low.** Keep it short and protect sleep rather than building a ten-step routine.

## ðŸ˜´ Protect Sleep and Recovery

Healthy sleep supports stress regulation and makes routines easier to follow. Set a realistic study cutoff, dim screens before bed and avoid sacrificing every night to revision. Exercise, regular meals and supportive conversations can also reduce stress, even though none is a standalone acne cure.

Do not respond to breakouts with restrictive dieting. Evidence around specific foods is mixed, and teens need adequate nutrition during growth and exams.
