## Step 4: Eye Cream (Optional)

The eye area is delicate and often shows the first signs of aging.

**Skip this if:** You're on a budget (your regular moisturizer works fine)

**Add this if:** You have specific concerns like dark circles or puffiness

Look for caffeine (for puffiness) or peptides (for fine lines).
