## ðŸ’„ Remove Makeup and Sunscreen at Night

Sleeping in makeup can leave oil, pigment and product residue on acne-prone skin. Remove it gently, then cleanse once. Double cleansing is optional, not a rule. If one mild cleanser removes everything without rubbing, an extra step is unnecessary.

Clean makeup brushes regularly and never share applicators. Choose products labeled oil-free or non-comedogenic, remembering that no label guarantees a perfect match for every person.
