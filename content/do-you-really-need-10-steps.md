The 10-step skincare routine became popular through Korean beauty trends—but is it really necessary?

Let’s break it down.

---

## 🤔 What Is the 10-Step Routine?

It typically includes:

- Cleanser  
- Toner  
- Essence  
- Serums  
- Moisturizer  
- Sunscreen  

…and more.

---

## ❌ The Problem

Too many products can:

- Irritate your skin  
- Cause breakouts  
- Waste money  

---

## ✅ What You Actually Need

A simple routine works best:

1. Cleanser  
2. Moisturizer  
3. Sunscreen

## 🧠 When Is More Okay?

Advanced routines are fine if:

- You understand your skin  
- You introduce products slowly  

---

## ⚖️ Simple vs Complex

| Simple | Complex |
|------|--------|
| Easy to follow | Hard to maintain |
| Affordable | Expensive |
| Less irritation | Higher risk |

---

## 🧠 Final Thoughts

You don’t need a 10-step routine to have great skin.

Simple + consistent always wins.
