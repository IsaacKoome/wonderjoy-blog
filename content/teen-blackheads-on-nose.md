## ðŸš« Skip Squeezing and Metal Extractors

Pressing the nose can rupture a pore, introduce bacteria and leave a dark mark or scar. Home extraction tools add pressure and are difficult to sterilize. Dermatologists can perform extraction when appropriate, but routine squeezing is not maintenance.

If the urge is hard to resist, move away from the mirror, shorten inspection time and cover an inflamed surface pimple with a hydrocolloid patch. A patch does not pull out deep blackheads.
