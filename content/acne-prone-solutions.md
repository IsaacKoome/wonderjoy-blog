## 2. Benzoyl Peroxide (For Inflammatory Acne)

Benzoyl peroxide kills acne-causing bacteria (P. acnes) and reduces inflammation. It's the most effective over-the-counter treatment for red, swollen pimples.

**Best for:** Red, inflamed pimples, cystic acne
**How to use:** Start with 2.5% concentration (just as effective as 10%, but less irritating)

**Pro tip:** Apply to clean skin, leave for 2-3 minutes, then rinse. This reduces irritation while maintaining effectiveness.

## 5. Patch Testing (Never Skip This)

Patch testing is the only way to know if a product will break you out before you put it all over your face.

**How to patch test:**
1. Apply a small amount behind your ear or on your jawline
2. Wait 24-48 hours
3. Check for redness, bumps, or irritation
4. If clear, try it on half your face for another week

**Pro tip:** Introduce only ONE new product at a time. If you start three things at once and break out, you won't know which one caused it.
