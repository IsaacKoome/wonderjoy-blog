## 🧴 The Only 3 Steps You Actually Need

### 1. Gentle Cleanser (Morning & Night)

Washing your face removes dirt, oil, and sweat that builds up during the day and overnight.

**What to look for:**
- "Gentle" or "for sensitive skin" on the label
- No harsh beads or scrubs for daily use
- Non-foaming or low-foaming formulas

**What to avoid:**
- Harsh soaps that leave your skin feeling tight
- Over-washing (twice a day is plenty)

**What to look for:**
- "Non-comedogenic" (won't clog pores)
- "Oil-free" or "gel" formula

### 3. Sunscreen (Every Morning)

This is the most important step for long-term skin health. Sun damage happens even on cloudy days, and it's one of the main causes of dark spots and scarring from acne.

**What to look for:**
- SPF 30 or higher
- "Oil-free" or "matte finish"
- Won't feel heavy or greasy

## ❌ Common Mistakes Teens Make

Avoid these and your skin will thank you:

| Mistake | Why It's Bad |
|---------|---------------|
| Popping pimples | Causes scarring and spreads bacteria |
| Using harsh scrubs daily | Irritates skin and makes acne worse |
| Skipping sunscreen | Darkens acne scars over time |
| Sleeping in makeup | Clogs pores and causes breakouts |
| Changing products too fast | Skin needs 4-6 weeks to show results |

---

## 🧠 Acne Facts Every Teen Should Know

**Fact 1:** Acne is not your fault. It's caused by hormones, genetics, and bacteria — not by being "dirty."

**Fact 2:** Toothpaste does NOT belong on pimples. It burns the skin and makes redness worse.

**Fact 3:** More products don't mean better results. A simple routine wins every time.

**Fact 4:** Stress and lack of sleep can trigger breakouts. Your skin reflects what's happening inside your body.

## 💡 Quick Morning Routine (60 Seconds)

1. Splash face with lukewarm water (or gentle cleanser if oily)
2. Apply oil-free moisturizer
3. Apply sunscreen

That's it. Seriously.

---

## 💡 Quick Night Routine (90 Seconds)

1. Gentle cleanser
2. Oil-free moisturizer
3. Spot treatment (if needed — benzoyl peroxide or salicylic acid)

---

## 🧠 Final Thoughts for Teens

You don't need to spend hours on skincare. You don't need 15 products. You don't need to compare your skin to filtered photos online.

Consistency beats complexity every time.

Start with the three steps. Do them every single day. Give it two months. Then decide if you need to add anything else.

Your skin is already doing its best. Just help it along with simple, gentle care.
