## 4 More Winter Skincare Tips

| **Tip** | **Why It Works** |
|---------|------------------|
| **Layer your hydration** | Toner → serum → moisturizer → facial oil (traps moisture) |
| **Don't skip sunscreen** | Winter sun still causes damage and dark spots |
| **Shower with lukewarm water** | Hot water strips natural oils |
| **Pat dry, don't rub** | Rubbing removes moisture and irritates skin |
