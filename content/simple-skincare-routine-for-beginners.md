Starting a skincare routine can feel overwhelming. With thousands of products and conflicting advice online, it’s easy to waste money and still not see results.

The truth is—you don’t need a complicated 10-step routine. A simple, consistent routine is all you need to get healthy, glowing skin.

---

## 🧠 Why Keep It Simple?

Many beginners make the mistake of using too many products at once. This can:

- Irritate your skin  
- Cause breakouts  
- Waste money  
- Make it hard to know what actually works  

Keeping things simple helps your skin adjust and gives you better results over time.

---

- Use a gentle, non-stripping cleanser

### 4. Optional: Treatment (Only If Needed)

Once your basic routine is stable, you can add:

- Acne treatments (salicylic acid)  
- Brightening serums (vitamin C)  
- Hydrating serums (hyaluronic acid)  

Start slow—don’t introduce everything at once.

---

## ⚠️ Common Beginner Mistakes

Avoid these:

- Using too many products  
- Switching products too often  
- Skipping sunscreen  
- Overwashing your face  

---

## ⏳ When Will You See Results?

Skincare takes time. Most people notice:

- Smoother skin in 1–2 weeks  
- Fewer breakouts in 3–4 weeks  
- Visible improvement in 6–8 weeks  

Consistency matters more than anything else.
