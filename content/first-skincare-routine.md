# The Perfect Morning Skincare Routine

Achieving glowing skin doesn't require a 10-step routine. Here's what actually works...

## Step 1: Gentle Cleanser

Start your morning with a pH-balanced, non-stripping cleanser. Avoid harsh sulfates that can strip your skin's natural oils.

## Step 2: Vitamin C Serum

Vitamin C is a powerful antioxidant that protects your skin from environmental damage and brightens your complexion.

## Step 3: Moisturizer

Even oily skin needs hydration. Choose a lightweight, non-comedogenic moisturizer that won't clog pores.

## Step 4: Sunscreen

This is the most important step. Use SPF 30 or higher every single day, rain or shine, to prevent premature aging and skin damage.

## Why This Routine Works

This routine is backed by dermatologists because it:
- Protects your skin barrier
- Provides essential nutrients
- Prevents sun damage
- Maintains hydration

Start with these 4 steps and you'll notice a difference in just 2 weeks!
