## What Are Dark Spots?

Dark spots occur when your skin produces excess melanin (the pigment that gives skin its color). This overproduction can be triggered by several factors:

- **Sun exposure** (most common cause)
- **Acne** (post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation)
- **Hormonal changes** (melasma)
- **Skin injuries** (cuts, burns, or irritation)
- **Aging** (cumulative sun damage over time)

## Common Mistakes That Make Dark Spots Worse

### ❌ Skipping Sunscreen
Without daily SPF, no treatment will work effectively.

**The fix:** Use SPF 30-50 every single day, rain or shine.

### ❌ Using Too Many Actives at Once
Combining vitamin C, retinol, and AHAs can damage your skin barrier.

**The fix:** Introduce ONE new product at a time. Wait 2-4 weeks before adding another.

### ❌ Picking at Your Skin
Every squeezed pimple leaves behind a dark mark that can last months.

**The fix:** Use pimple patches and keep your hands off your face.
