## âšª Treat the Bumps Without Overdoing It

A salicylic acid cleanser or leave-on product may help small blackheads and whiteheads. Start a few times weekly, following age directions. Do not combine it immediately with a scrub, peel pads and a harsh acne wash.

Red, inflamed pimples may respond better to benzoyl peroxide, while persistent widespread acne may need adapalene or prescription care. A pharmacist or clinician can help match treatment to the bump type.
